FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What services does NannyWoofWoof offer?

A: NannyWoofWoof offers a range of grooming services, including bathing, brushing, hair trimming, nail clipping, ear cleaning, and more. We also provide dog boarding and daycare services in our pet-friendly facility.


Is NannyWoofWoof open for walk-ins, or do I need to make an appointment?

A: We recommend making an appointment to ensure we can accommodate your pet's grooming needs effectively. Walk-ins are subject to availability.


What is the cost of grooming services at NannyWoofWoof?

A: Grooming prices vary depending on the size of your pet, their breed, and the specific services required. Please contact us for a personalized quote or visit our website for pricing details.


Is NannyWoofWoof suitable for nervous or anxious pets?

A: Yes, NannyWoofWoof is experienced in handling nervous or anxious pets. Our team is trained to provide a calming and stress-free environment for all pets, ensuring their well-being and comfort.


Do you offer breed-specific grooming styles?

A: Yes, our professional groomers are skilled in breed-specific styling. Whether your dog needs a standard cut or a breed-specific trim, we can accommodate your requests.


Are there any vaccination requirements for dogs staying at NannyWoofWoof's boarding facility?

A: Yes, all dogs staying in our boarding facility must be up-to-date on their vaccinations. This requirement helps ensure the safety and health of all pets in our care.


Does NannyWoofWoof offer any additional perks for pets during their stay?

A: Yes, we offer additional services such as playtime, walks, and spa treatments to enhance your pet's experience during their stay. You can customize your pet's stay to suit their preferences and needs.


If you have any more questions or need further information about our services, please don't hesitate to contact us. We're here to provide the best care for your beloved pets at NannyWoofWoof in Inglewood, CA.
